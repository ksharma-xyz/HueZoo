The science behind the game

WHAT IS ΔE?

ΔE (Delta E) is the scientific unit for how different two colors look to the human eye — not a camera, not a screen calibrator. Your actual eyes. The smaller the ΔE, the harder it is to tell them apart.

ΔE 5.0 BEGINNER

Plainly visible to everyone. The difference is obvious.

How it works

SIMPLE.
BRUTAL.

Six color swatches. Five are identical. One is slightly different. Pick it before you run out of chances. That's the whole game.

STEP 01
01
Spot the odd color
Six swatches appear. Five are the same color. One is a few shades off — sometimes obviously, sometimes nearly invisible. Tap the one that looks different.
STEP 02
02
Each round narrows
Get it right and the ΔE gap shrinks next round. It goes from "I can clearly see it" to "wait, are these actually different?" You'll know which you're in.
STEP 03
03
Your ΔE score is your rank
When you miss, your score is the ΔE at that point. Professional colorists work below ΔE 1.0. Most people tap out at ΔE 3.0. Where do you fall?
