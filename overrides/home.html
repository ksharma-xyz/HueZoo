How it works

SIMPLE.
BRUTAL.

Six color swatches. Five are identical. One is slightly different. Pick it before you run out of chances. That's the whole game.

01
Spot the odd color
Six swatches appear. Five are the same color. One is a few shades off — sometimes obviously, sometimes nearly invisible. Tap the one that looks different.
02
Each round gets harder
Get it right and the difference shrinks next round. The gap goes from "I can clearly see it" to "wait, are these actually different colors?" You'll know which you're in.
03
Your ΔE score is your rank
When you miss, your score is the ΔE at that point. Professional colorists work below ΔE 1.0. Most people tap out at ΔE 3.0. Where do you fall?
The science

WHAT IS ΔE?

ΔE (Delta E) is the scientific unit for how different two colors look to a human eye. Not to a camera — to an actual human. The smaller the number, the harder it is to tell them apart.

ΔE 5.0 Obvious — everyone sees this
ΔE 2.0 Requires focus
