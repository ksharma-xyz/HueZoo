    Huezoo — Privacy Policy¶

    Last updated: April 7, 2026

    We believe in simple, honest privacy. Huezoo is a game — it does not need to know who you are, and it does not try to find out.

    1. Information We Do Not Collect¶

    You do not need an account to play Huezoo. We do not collect or store:

    • Your name, email address, or phone number
    • Any data that links your identity to your gameplay
    • Payment or financial information (purchases are processed entirely by Google Play or the App Store)

    2. Data Stored on Your Device¶

    Huezoo stores game progress, settings, and usage history locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time from within the app or via your device's app settings.

    3. Advertising¶

    Huezoo displays ads via Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may access your device's Advertising ID. On iOS, you will be asked for permission before any advertising identifier is accessed, via the App Tracking Transparency prompt.

    You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

    • iOS — Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
    • Android — Google Settings → Ads → Delete advertising ID

    Ad delivery is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

    4. Analytics & Crash Reporting (Anonymous)¶

    To improve stability and gameplay, we collect limited, non-identifiable technical data via Firebase (Google):

    • Usage analytics — which features are used and how, aggregated and never linked to you personally
    • Crash reports — device model and OS version when the app crashes, so we can fix bugs
    • Performance data — app speed and reliability metrics

    No personally identifiable information is included in any of these reports. Analytics data is processed by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

    5. In-App Purchases¶

    Huezoo offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock the full game. All payment processing is handled by Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We never see or store your payment details.

    6. Third-Party Services¶

    Service Purpose
    Google AdMob Advertising
    Firebase (Google) Analytics & crash reporting
    Google Play Billing / StoreKit In-app purchases

    We do not sell your data to third parties, use data brokers, or share your data beyond what is described above.

    7. Your Rights¶

    • Clear local data — game progress and settings can be cleared from within the app at any time
    • Advertising opt-out — disable personalised ads via iOS or Android settings (see Section 3)
    • Analytics opt-out — on iOS via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; on Android via Google Settings → Ads

    Since we do not collect personal identifiers, there is no personal profile to access, export, or delete.

    8. Changes to this Privacy Policy¶

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
